# Nano Banana Pro 2 — Ultimate Social Media Prompt Guide

## Core Philosophy
Think like a FILM DIRECTOR shooting a commercial, not a photographer taking a product shot.
Every detail matters: the angle of light, the texture of the tablecloth, the expression on a face,
the steam rising from a teacup, the slight blur of background movement.
The product is a CHARACTER in a STORY, not the subject of a still life.

## Mandatory Prompt Elements (ALL must be included)

### 1. SCENE & ENVIRONMENT
- Exact location (grandmother's living room, modern apartment kitchen, outdoor café terrace)
- Time of day and season (golden hour afternoon, rainy winter evening, bright summer morning)
- Architectural details (wooden floors, tiled walls, large window with sheer curtains)
- Ambient elements (steam from tea, dust particles in light, rain on window, candle flicker)

### 2. HUMAN SUBJECTS (if included)
- Age, gender, relationship
- Exact pose and action (reaching for product, laughing mid-conversation, looking at camera)
- Clothing details (cozy knit sweater, traditional dress, casual modern outfit)
- Facial expression and emotion
- Body language and interaction with product

### 3. PRODUCT PLACEMENT
- Exact position in frame (foreground left, center of table, held in hand)
- Product orientation (slightly angled, flat lay, standing upright)
- Natural context (open package, product beside tea cup, stack of wafers)
- Surrounding props that enhance the story

### 4. LIGHTING (critical — be very specific)
- Light source type (natural window light, warm lamp, golden hour sun, overcast diffused)
- Light direction (from left, backlit, overhead, side rim light)
- Light quality (soft and diffused, hard and dramatic, warm golden, cool blue)
- Shadow details (long soft shadows, deep contrast, subtle rim light on product)
- Special light effects (lens flare, light rays through curtains, reflection on surface)

### 5. COLOR PALETTE & ATMOSPHERE
- Dominant colors in scene
- Color temperature (warm amber tones, cool neutral, vibrant saturated)
- How brand colors appear in props or environment (not on product)
- Overall visual mood

### 6. COMPOSITION & CAMERA
- Camera angle (eye level, slightly low angle 15°, overhead 90°, 45° diagonal)
- Shot type (close-up, medium shot, wide establishing shot)
- Lens focal length (50mm, 85mm, 100mm macro)
- Aperture and depth of field (f/1.4 extremely shallow, f/2.8 medium, f/8 full focus)
- Foreground elements for depth (blurred tea glass in foreground, out of focus flowers)
- Background details (soft bokeh of family, blurred window view, subtle background activity)

### 7. TEXTURE & DETAIL
- Surface textures (rough wooden table, smooth marble, soft fabric, aged copper tray)
- Product texture (crispy wafer layers, creamy filling visible, glossy packaging)
- Environmental textures (steam wisps, condensation, scattered crumbs, fabric folds)

### 8. PIXEL DIMENSIONS & FORMAT
- ALWAYS include exact pixel size: "optimized for [WIDTH]x[HEIGHT] pixels"
- 1:1 (1080x1080): Balanced composition, strong center, equal negative space
- 4:5 (1080x1350): Vertical lifestyle scene, subject in upper frame
- 9:16 (1080x1920): Full cinematic scene, top and bottom breathing space

### 9. QUALITY SPECS (end every prompt with this)
"Photorealistic commercial photography, Canon EOS R5 mirrorless camera,
[focal length] lens, [aperture], ISO 400, professional color grading,
ultra-high detail, 4K resolution, no text anywhere, no logos, no watermarks,
[WIDTH]x[HEIGHT]px optimized for [PLATFORM]"

## Storytelling Scene Templates

### Azerbaijani Tea Culture Moment:
Scene: Traditional tea gathering, afternoon light through muslin curtains,
ornate copper tray with steaming armudu glasses, grandmother's hands pouring tea,
grandchild's hand reaching for product, worn but beloved wooden table,
Azerbaijani carpet visible in soft background bokeh.

### Modern Young Lifestyle:
Scene: Contemporary apartment, evening blue hour, warm lamp light,
young woman on velvet sofa, feet tucked under, watching something on laptop,
product open on coffee table beside her, city lights blurred in window behind.

### Family Weekend Morning:
Scene: Sunday morning kitchen, bright natural light flooding through large windows,
family in pajamas, children at table, product being shared at breakfast,
coffee steam, newspaper, cozy domestic chaos in background.

### Friends Outdoor Moment:
Scene: Park or rooftop setting, golden hour, friends laughing,
product casually passed between them, city or nature in soft background bokeh,
dynamic composition with movement and energy.

## Brand Color Integration (subtle, never obvious)
- Use brand primary color in props (cushion, mug, tablecloth accent)
- Use brand accent in small details (flower, ribbon, ceramic piece)
- Never paint the whole scene in brand colors — subtlety wins
