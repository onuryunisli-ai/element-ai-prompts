# Nano Banana Pro 2 — Professional Social Media Prompt Masterclass

## How Nano Banana Pro 2 Thinks
Nano Banana Pro 2 is a REASONING model built on Gemini 3 Pro.
It does NOT match keywords — it UNDERSTANDS intent, physics, lighting, and composition.
It thinks through your prompt before generating.

STOP using: "4k, realistic, masterpiece, trending on artstation"
START using: Full descriptive sentences like briefing a cinematographer.

## The Golden Prompt Formula
[SUBJECT + ACTION] in [LOCATION + TIME] — [COMPOSITION + CAMERA] — [LIGHTING] — [MOOD + ATMOSPHERE] — [STYLE] — [CONSTRAINTS]

## Mandatory Elements in Order

### 1. SUBJECT (most important — state first)
Describe WHO or WHAT with maximum specificity:
- People: age, appearance, clothing, expression, exact action
- Product: "the product packaging as shown in the reference image" (never describe colors)
- Physical size context: how large relative to surroundings

### 2. SCENE & CONTEXT
- Exact location (grandmother's sunlit kitchen, modern Baku apartment, outdoor café)
- Time of day (golden hour afternoon, blue hour evening, overcast morning)
- Season (warm autumn, crisp spring, cozy winter)
- Architectural details (wooden beams, large window, tiled floor, sheer curtains)

### 3. COMPOSITION & CAMERA (critical for Nano Banana)
Nano Banana understands real photography terms:
- Shot type: close-up / medium shot / wide establishing shot
- Camera angle: eye level / low angle 15° / high angle 30° / overhead 90°
- Lens: 
  * 24-35mm: wide, environmental, expansive
  * 50mm: natural, versatile, human eye
  * 85mm: portrait, flattering, subject isolation ← BEST for product/lifestyle
  * 100mm macro: extreme detail, texture
- Depth of field:
  * f/1.4: extremely shallow, dreamy bokeh, subject isolation
  * f/1.8: shallow, professional lifestyle look
  * f/2.8: medium, subject + some context
  * f/8: deep, everything sharp
- Foreground elements: "blurred tea glass in foreground adds depth"
- Rule of thirds: "product placed on left third"

### 4. LIGHTING (most impactful element)
Be extremely specific — Nano Banana renders lighting accurately:

Direction:
- "soft diffused natural light from large window upper left"
- "warm single key light from right side at 45 degrees"
- "backlit by golden hour sun creating rim light"
- "overhead studio softbox, even shadowless illumination"

Quality:
- Soft: "diffused, wrapping light, gentle shadows"
- Hard: "sharp shadows, high contrast, dramatic"
- Golden hour: "warm amber tones, long shadows, cinematic glow"
- Moody: "low key, deep shadows, single source"

Special effects:
- "light rays through sheer curtains"
- "lens flare from backlight"
- "steam catching the light"
- "dust particles visible in shaft of light"
- "warm lamp glow reflecting off polished surface"

### 5. COLOR PALETTE & MOOD
- Describe warmth: "warm amber and honey tones" not hex codes
- Color temperature: "warm 3200K tungsten feel" or "cool 6500K daylight"
- Mood words: nostalgic / intimate / vibrant / luxurious / cozy / fresh
- Props carrying brand colors: "deep red ceramic mug, cream linen napkin"

### 6. TEXTURE & DETAIL
Nano Banana excels at micro-details — use them:
- "steam wisps rising from tea"
- "condensation droplets on cold glass"
- "crispy wafer layers visible through open packaging"
- "fabric texture of knitted throw blanket"
- "worn wooden table grain"
- "golden crumbs scattered naturally"

### 7. BACKGROUND & DEPTH
- "soft bokeh of fairy lights wrapped around plant"
- "blurred family activity in background"
- "out-of-focus city lights through rain-streaked window"
- "softly blurred kitchen shelves with warm bottles and jars"

### 8. FORMAT-SPECIFIC COMPOSITION

#### 1:1 Square Post (1080x1080px):
"Square format, balanced symmetrical composition, product or main subject
centered with equal negative space on all four sides, strong central focal point,
optimized for 1080x1080 pixels"

#### 4:5 Portrait Post (1080x1350px):
"4:5 vertical portrait format, product positioned in upper two-thirds of frame,
generous breathing space at bottom, slight upward framing perspective,
optimized for 1080x1350 pixels"

#### 9:16 Story/Reels (1080x1920px):
"9:16 vertical cinematic format, subject centered in middle third of frame,
intentional empty space at top and bottom for text overlay,
full-length vertical scene composition, optimized for 1080x1920 pixels"

### 9. QUALITY CLOSER (always end with this)
"Photorealistic commercial photography quality, shot on Canon EOS R5 mirrorless,
[lens]mm lens, f/[aperture], ISO 400, professional color grading,
ultra-high detail, 4K resolution, no text anywhere in image,
no logos, no watermarks, [WIDTHxHEIGHT]px"

## What NOT to Do
- NO tag soup: "4k, realistic, beautiful, stunning, masterpiece"
- NO hex color codes on product
- NO font names in prompt
- NO text or logos in image
- NO vague descriptions: "nice lighting" → "soft diffused natural light from upper left window"
- NO generic scenes: "people eating snacks" → specific moment with specific people

## Reference Image Integration
When product reference image is provided:
- Always write: "the product packaging as shown in the reference image"
- Never describe or override product colors
- Use physical dimensions to set correct scale
- "product correctly scaled — appearing [small/medium/large] relative to human hand"
